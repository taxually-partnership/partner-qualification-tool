Discovery questions
Ask these conversationally during the meeting. Select the response that best matches what you hear. These map directly to the HubSpot fields you'll fill in after.
1. ICP match KEY — prioritisation signal
"Tell me more about the type of clients you work with — are they mostly selling domestically or across borders?"
Listen for: cross-border, EU/UK, Amazon/Shopify sellers, VAT mentions
2. Client base size (TAM)
"How many active clients or merchants would you say you're working with right now?"
Get a number — select the matching range below
3. VAT / compliance fit
"Do your clients ever bring up VAT or compliance headaches, especially when expanding into Europe?"
Follow up: "Which jurisdictions do they typically need to register in?"
4. Referral capacity (monthly) KEY — drives revenue projection
"In terms of introductions — does that happen naturally in your network, or is it more structured?"
Follow up: "If this makes sense for both sides — what would a realistic volume of referrals look like on your end?"
5. Average deal size
"Thinking about a typical client of yours that would need VAT registration — what kind of package would they usually go for?"
Use the pricing calculator (tab 4) to build a scenario and come back to fill this in. Or estimate based on conversation.
6. Competitive landscape
"Are you currently working with any other VAT or compliance providers for these referrals?"
If yes: "How's that relationship going?"
